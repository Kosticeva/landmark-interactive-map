The Natural Heritage Map

The Natural Heritage Map is an application which allows you to keep track of all the wonders of nature, whether it is a mountain top, a lake or some form of land formation.

Basic elements of the Natural Heritage Map are

  • landmarks,
  • landmark types and
  • tags.

Each landmark is unique and has its own ID, name and landmark type, as well as some other optional data.

Landmark types are also unique and have their own ID, name and picture.

Tags are optional and placed on landmarks. They are also unique and have their own ID and color.


You can place your landmarks on a map, and move them around if you want.

Also, if you don't like some part of your landmark/landmark type/tag, you can always edit it, or even delete it.

But first, to use this application, you need to create your own account.
