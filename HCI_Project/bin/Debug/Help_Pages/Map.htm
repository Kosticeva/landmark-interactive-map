All about the Map

There are 3 main parts of the map:

  • The Background, where you can put your landmark;
  • The Toolbar, which can be personalized through the menu, and
  • Landmarks, presented as squares filled with the picture you selected for the landmark.

Drag and Drop

Everything that is placed on the background is movable. You can drag it around the map by selecting it, dragging it with your mouse and releasing it at the desired position.

To select a landmark or a toolbar, you have to click it with your left mouse button. If you have clicked a landmark, its border will be colored with green and red.

You can start dragging the desired item by pressing left mouse button on it and holding the button while you drag the mouse pointer around the background.

To drop your landmark, you must release your left mouse button at the desired position. The same principle is used for positioning your toolbar.

You can change your map background from the menu option 'Change Map'.
