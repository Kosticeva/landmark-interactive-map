Using the Natural Heritage Map

You have succesfully logged into the application, so you are ready to create your own landmarks!

The main window of the application has two main parts:

  1. The menu
  2. The map

Menu

The menu is the place where you can choose the action you want to perform. It is placed on the upper edge of the window. From the menu you can create new landmarks , landmark types, tags and also manage already created items. Also, from the menu you can manage the position of the landmarks placed on the map, save your current map, exit the application or view all landmarks.

Map

The map is the large area below the menu where you can place your landmarks. Landmarks that are placed on the map are presented as squares with white borders and filled with the picture selected for the landmark. You can move landmarks around the map, or use Cut, Copy, Paste and Delete controls.
