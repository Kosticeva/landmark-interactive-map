Signing in The Natural Heritage Map

To use the Natural Heritage Map, you have to create an account for yourself.

Every user is identified by his unique username and password.

To navigate through fields on the dialog, you can use your pointing device or the TAB key on your keyboard.


Username

Every user has a unique username. First step at signing in is choosing your username. Username is entered in the upper text field.

As you enter your username, message about the validity of your username will appear above of the mentioned text field, which will inform you if another user has the entered username.

Once your username is unique, the message will dissapear.

E-mail

If you like, you can enter your e-mail address into the middle field.

Password

Your password is the second data needed for signing into your account. Password is entered in the lower text field.

There are no limitations to the form of your password: it can be as long as you want and it can contain any character you like.

Signing in

If you have entered valid data, you will be able to start using the application. To create your account and sign in, click the 'Accept' button placed in the lower right corner of the dialog or press Control+Enter keys on your keyboard. After clicking the button, a Hello message will appear on the screen.

Canceling

If you have an account or if you wish to cancel creating your account, you can click the 'Cancel' button placed in the lower left corner of the screen or press Control+Escape keys on your keyboard.


If you wish to exit the application, you can click the 'X' button on the top right corner of the dialog, or press Alt+F4 keys on your keyboard.
