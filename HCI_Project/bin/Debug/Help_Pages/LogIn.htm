Logging in The Natural Heritage Map

If you have already used The Natural Heritage Map, you should have your own account.

To access your account and continue where you started, you should enter your username and your password into the appropriate fields on the form.

To navigate through fields on the dialog, you can use your pointing device or the TAB key on your keyboard.


Username

When you signed in for using this application, you entered a unique username which defines you. First step at logging in is entering your username. Username is entered in the upper text field.

As you enter your username, message about the validity of your username will appear above of the mentioned text field, which will inform you if your username is valid.

Once you enter your username, the message will dissapear.

Password

Your password is the second data needed for logging into your account. Password is entered in the lower text field.

If you have already entered your username, you will receive information about the validity of your password as you are typing. The message will appear below the mentioned text field.

Once you enter correct password for your username, the message will dissapear.

Logging in

If you have entered valid data, you will be able to start using the application. To log in, click the 'Log In' button placed in the lower right corner of the dialog or press the Control+Enter keys on your keyboard.

Signing in

If you do not have an account, you have to sign in first. To sign in, click the 'Sign In' button placed in the lower left corner of the dialog or press Control+Escape keys on your keyboard.


If you wish to exit the application, you can click the 'X' button on the top right corner of the dialog, or press Alt+F4 keys on your keyboard.
